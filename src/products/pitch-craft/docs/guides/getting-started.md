# Getting Started with Pitch Craft

This guide will walk you through the basics of using Pitch Craft to create and present your pitches.

## Creating a New Project

1.  From the projects page, click the "Create New" button.
2.  Enter a name for your project and click "Create."

## Adding and Editing Slides

*   To add a new slide, click the "Add Slide" button in the left-hand sidebar.
*   To reorder slides, simply drag and drop them in the sidebar.
*   To delete a slide, hover over it in the sidebar and click the trash icon.

## Adding and Editing Text

1.  Click the "Add Text" button in the toolbar.
2.  A new text box will be added to the canvas.
3.  You can then click and drag the text box to move it around.
4.  To edit the text, simply double-click on the text box.

## Adding and Editing Images

1.  Click the "Add Image" button in the toolbar.
2.  You can then either upload an image from your computer or select one from the media library.
3.  Once the image is added to the canvas, you can resize and move it as needed.

## Presenting Your Pitch

*   To enter presentation mode, click the "Present" button in the top-right corner of the editor.
*   You can then use the arrow keys to navigate through your slides.

## Saving and Sharing Your Pitch

*   Your pitch is automatically saved as you work.
*   To share your pitch, you can either export it as a PDF or share a link to the presentation.
